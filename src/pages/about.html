How come I make this site?

So I saw this neocities site, and I thought it was pretty creative (though made me feel uneasy). Then I look it up more of neocities sites like cinni's, lunospace, and more I didn't mention. They all have their own personality. But the common thing of them is that they are written with good ol' regular HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. So I decided to hop in :).

Since I normally use framework like SvelteKit to build a website, this gets me back to the basic. I write this website in vanilla HTML, JavaScript, SASS (a CSS preprocessor), and a few libraries like Prettier. It feels kinda weird when you've gotten used to something that is, at first, feels hard to use, and then once I'm back at where I was, I quickly realize why I quit writing stuff in vanilla, lol.
